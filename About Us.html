M&T

We started M&T because we felt there wasn't enough attention towards men fashion styles.

That's why, in order to observe unique fashion trends, we traveled to Japan, Singapore and Germany.

Our journey began in 2014 with only three of us in a garage. Now we stand proud in 4 countries.

Along the way, we recruited skilled artisans, which led us to our victory in Met Gala 2020.

We want to produce trend-shifting fashion wear that brings out the best out of every man.
